KALKI

THE LAST AVATAR
AI-GENERATED TRAILER
Watch Trailer

KALKI is an AI-generated epic reimagining of Vishnu's final avatar, Kalki, rising in a dystopian world to battle ultimate evil. This groundbreaking project combines ancient mythology with cutting-edge AI animation technology to create a visually stunning and emotionally resonant experience.

Project Details

Concept

Reimagining the Kalki avatar in a cyberpunk dystopia where technology and spirituality collide in an epic struggle between good and evil.

Technology

KALKI is crafted using AI-generated animation, Unreal Engine 5, and neural rendering to fuse ancient mythology with next-gen cinematic technology..

Duration

KALKI was developed over 2 months, combining AI pre-visualization, animation, and post-production to bring this mythic vision to life.

Behind the Scenes

Our team spent months researching Hindu mythology and developing the visual language for this project. Using advanced AI animation tools, we created a unique aesthetic that blends traditional iconography with futuristic elements.

The character of Kalki was generated using a combination of text-to-image AI models and custom-trained neural networks that interpreted ancient descriptions through a modern lens.
